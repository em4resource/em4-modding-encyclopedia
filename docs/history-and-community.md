# Emergency 4 / 911: First Responders History and Community Context

Emergency 4: Global Fighters for Life released in Europe in 2006. In North America, the game is known as 911: First Responders and released in 2007.

The game puts the player in the role of an incident commander managing police, fire, EMS, and technical rescue units during accidents, fires, crimes, disasters, and rescue situations.

## Why It Became a Modding Platform

Emergency 4 stayed active because the game exposes many systems to modders:

- Vehicles
- Personnel
- Equipment
- Maps
- Models
- Textures
- Prototypes
- Scripts
- Missions
- Freeplay events
- UI icons
- Audio
- Unit browser entries

## Major Community Mods

Well-known Emergency 4 / 911: First Responders mods include:

- Los Angeles Mod
- New York City Mod
- Manhattan Mod
- Winterberg Mod
- London Mod
- Montana Mod
- West Falls Township Mod
- West Lampeter Mod
- Bieberfelde Multiplayer

The Los Angeles Mod is especially important for American-style modding. Version 2.1 advertised more than 50 playable units, a new freeplay map, detailed models, ten missions, new sirens, directional lights, fire stations, a police station, a hospital, weapons, barricades, bomb robots, motorcycles, multiplayer support, and English voices.
